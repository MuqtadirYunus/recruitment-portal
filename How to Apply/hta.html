Application Guide

Ensure you are completing the application fromcareers.nnpcgroup.com.

NNPC Limited Careers home.

  • Ensure you have a functioning email address you can access.
  • Ensure you have a functioning phone number you can be reached on.
  • Go through the How-To-Apply Page.
  • Read through the eligibility criteria.
  • Understand the Job Requirements.
  • Ensure you have all the required documents to upload ready.
  • Birth Certificate
  • Indigene Certificate
  • Passport Photograph
  • Educational Certificates (Secondary School and Tertiary Institution only).
  • NIN
  • Professional Membership Certificates where applicable
  • NYSC Certificate

Select your preferred job category.

  • On the careers.nnpcgroup.com:
    • go to careers
    • select either Graduate Trainee or Experienced Hire
    • Go through the eligibility criteria.
    • click on the apply button.

Click on "Apply Now".

  • For EH:
  • Click on the explore button.
  • Review available jobs by skill pool.
  • Click on preferred skill pool e.g. Security.
  • View available jobs and review job objective, job responsibility, educational qualification, job competence, experience, and age limit.
  • Select your preferred job and click "Apply Now".

Experienced hire job page.

  • Register with the following information:
  • First Name, Last Name and email and password.
  • Accept terms & conditions and privacy policy.

Registration Page

  • Complete Bio data page (Graduate Trainee & Experienced Hire):
  • First Name
  • Middle Name
  • Last Name
  • Gender
  • Upload Birth Certificate
  • Marital Status
  • State Origin
  • LGA
  • Upload Indigene Certificate
  • NIN
  • Phone
  • Emergency Number
  • Email
  • Permanent Home Address
  • Residential Address (If same as Permanent Home Address, enter it once.)
  • Choose Preferred CBT location
  • Any special need? If yes, specify
  • Passport Photograph

Bio Data page

  • Primary School Name and Year of Graduation
  • Secondary School Name and Year Graduation
  • Upload SSCE (Max 2 fields)
  • Higher Institution (Degree/HND)
  • Class of Degree (1st class, 2.1, 2.2 with Masters, HND distinction, upper credit with Masters)
  • Upload higher institution certificate

Education page

  • For Experienced Hire (EH):
  • Click on the explore button
  • Review available jobs by skill pool
  • Click on preferred skill pool e.g., Security
  • View available jobs and review job objective, job responsibility, educational qualification, job competence, experience, and age limit
  • Select your preferred job and click "Apply Now"
  • Enter the following under institution details:
  • Course of Study
  • Upload Higher Institution Certificate
  • NYSC Certificate Number
  • NYSC Year
  • Upload NYSC Certificate/Exemption
